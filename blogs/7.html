Top One Percent Knocking the Doors of Kellogg!

Kellogg School of Management at Northwestern University is one of the most popular business schools in the United States. It has been ranked number three in the world for an MBA and number one for a MBA in Marketing. The program boasts of a double majors in marketing, world renowned faculty, revolutionary teaching methodologies, a huge campus with an array of facilities and a unique curriculum.

Top recruiters like Starbucks, Walmart, Colgate-Palmolive, Mars, Nike, PepsiCo, Unilever, Adobe Systems, Google, Amazon and Apple flock the college to pick marketing geniuses. The institute offers specializations in accounting, economics, entrepreneurship, finance, general management, health care administration, leadership, marketing, production/operations management, organizational behavior, portfolio management, public administration, public policy, real estate, quantitative analysis/statistics and operations research.
